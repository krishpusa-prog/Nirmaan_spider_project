Spider-Man is a fictional Marvel Comics character with superhuman strength and agility, who shoots webs from his wrists to swing and fight crime after being bitten by a radioactive spider. His real name is Peter Parker, and he uses his abilities to protect people from criminals and supervillains, warning him of danger with a "spider sense". Known for his red and blue costume, he possesses the ability to climb walls and is a prominent superhero in the Marvel universe.
